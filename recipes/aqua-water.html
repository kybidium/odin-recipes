Aqua Watermelonis

Description

Said by ancient peoples to be the Drink of Kings, Aqua Watermelonis on a hot summer day is sure to cool you down. Indeed, this elegant recipe is meant for true royalty to enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups diced watermelon
  • 2 cups water
  • (Optional) sweetener (aspartame, sugar, etc.) to taste

Materials

  • A pitcher
  • A blender

Steps

  1. Combine the ingredients in the blender
  2. Blend the ingredients until smooth
  3. Pour the mixture into the pitcher
  4. Enjoy!
