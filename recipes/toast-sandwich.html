Toast Sandwich

Description

The best-kept secret of England, a toast sandwich is sure to satisfy your sandwich needs.

Ingredients

  • 3 slices bread
  • Butter (to taste)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Pepper (to taste)

Materials

  • Toaster
  • Table or butter knife for spreading butter

Steps

  1. Place 1 slice bread into the toaster; toast it to taste
  2. Extract the slice of bread from the toaster; spread the butter onto the slice (as desired), then add salt and pepper (as desired)
  3. Carefully place the toasted slice of bread between the two other slices of bread as one would with a sandwich
  4. Enjoy!
