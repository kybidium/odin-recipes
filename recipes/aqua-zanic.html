Aqua Zanica

For the aspiring renaissance man

Description

DISCLAIMER: this recipe is NOT meant for ingestion. Do not attempt if you are under the age of 21.

Known to the Dinikiites as poisan sharlatani, Aqua Zanica is the fabled elixir of the world's greatest economists, mathematicians, chess players, and pool experts. "Rhoshted over the fhinest khoals" and "aged for dhekhades in a barrel",this fine drink will delight you for sure.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup 120+ proof ethyl alcohol (perfume, lab-grade, etc. as desired)
  • 1/4 cup New York Water
  • Coals
  • Ice

Materials

  • A scottish crucible
  • A lighter or source of fire
  • A stirring rod
  • A barrel
  • A glass/cup (for drinking)

Steps

  1. Pour the alcohol and water into the crucible
  2. Light the coals on fire and heat the crucible over the coals
  3. Pour the crucible's mixture along with coal (to taste) in the barrel; seal the barrel
  4. Age the mixture for 10+ years; pour the mixture into the glass/cup
  5. Enjoy!
